Durga Puja, 2022

প্রথম দেখা — First Meeting

Amidst the dhak beats and fragrance of shiuli flowers during Kolkata's grandest Durga Puja, a chance meeting at a pandal hopping trail turned into hours of adda over mishti doi and phuchka. The city of joy brought two hearts together.

Poila Boisakh, 2024

ভালোবাসা — Falling in Love

From early morning walks along the Hooghly to late-night conversations over cups of Darjeeling chai, each season deepened what began as friendship into something neither could live without. Kolkata's monsoons became their love story's backdrop.
